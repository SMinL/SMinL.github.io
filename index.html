About

I am an Assistant Professor and Postdoctoral Researcher at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), supervised by Prof. Min Yang. My research focuses on natural language processing, large language models, and data mining. Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (2023–2024), where I worked on data visualization generation and intelligent data analysis. This work was supported by the Hong Kong Innovation and Technology Fund (ITF), supervised by Prof. Chen Jason Zhang. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology from the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) (2017–2023), advised by Prof. Jungang Xu. I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University of Science and Technology (2013–2017), where I ranked 2/172 and was recommended for direct Ph.D. admission to CAS.
