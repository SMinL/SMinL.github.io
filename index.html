About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), affiliated with the Shenzhen Key Laboratory for High-Performance Data Mining, which is led by Prof. Min Yang. My research focuses on natural language processing and data mining, with current interests in automated scientific discovery and multi-agent collaboration.
